About Dr. Kalam

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam (1931–2015) was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. Revered as the "Missile Man of India", he played a crucial role in India's space and defense programs. His dedication to science and youth made him a beloved figure across the country.

Major Achievements

  • Chief architect of India’s nuclear missile programs.
  • President of India (2002–2007), known as the “People’s President”.
  • Authored bestsellers like Wings of Fire, Ignited Minds.
  • Recipient of Bharat Ratna, Padma Vibhushan, and Padma Bhushan.
  • Inspired millions through speeches and dedication to education.
